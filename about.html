About Our School

Kilifi High Vision Academy is a CBC-focused private primary school located in the heart of Kilifi. We are dedicated to providing holistic, learner-centered education in a caring and stimulating environment.

Our Mission

To nurture responsible, confident, and skilled learners who will contribute positively to society through quality CBC education.

Our Vision

To be the leading private primary school in coastal Kenya — producing globally competitive students with strong values.
