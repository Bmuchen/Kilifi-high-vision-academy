Our History

Kilifi High Vision Academy was founded with a dream — to bring quality and affordable education to the children of Kilifi and its surroundings. What started as a small initiative in modest classrooms has grown into one of the most respected private schools in the county.

Since our inception, we have embraced the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) and invested in modern learning resources, technology, and highly trained teachers. Our growth story is built on discipline, academic performance, and strong partnerships with parents.

Today, our school continues to empower students to thrive not only in academics but also in values, creativity, and life skills that prepare them for the future.
