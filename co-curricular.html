Core Curriculum

At Kilifi High Vision Academy, our CBC-aligned core subjects equip learners with 21st-century skills, values, and competencies for real-world success.

Language

English, Kiswahili, and Sign Language to enhance communication and literacy skills.

Mathematics

Developing logical thinking and problem-solving through real-life application.

Science & Tech

Exploration of scientific concepts and technology integration to foster curiosity.

Social Studies

Understanding our society, environment, and civic responsibilities.

Creative Arts

Music, visual arts, and drama to spark innovation and self-expression.

Digital Literacy

Basic computing, coding, and responsible digital citizenship.

Religious Education

Values and moral guidance from Christian, Islamic, or Hindu teachings.

Life Skills

Guidance, hygiene, nutrition, and personal safety education.
